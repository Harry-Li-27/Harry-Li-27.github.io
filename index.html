Yuecheng Li

First year Ph.D. student at USC

My primary research interests encompass Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Probabilistic Machine Learning Models. I am currently working under the guidance of Professor Nicolas Schweighofer at the University of Southern California, focusing on Bayesian Modeling and its applications in Motor Learning. Previously, I collaborated with Professor Laurent Itti on projects related to Continual Learning and Transfer Learning. My academic journey includes an M.S. degree in Computer Science from the University of Southern California and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign. I have contributed to significant publications, including "CLR: Channel-wise Lightweight Reprogramming for Continual Learning" presented at ICCV 2023 and "Lightweight Learner for Shared Knowledge Lifelong Learning" published in TMLR. My work in transfer and continual learning has achieved state-of-the-art performance, surpassing existing benchmarks.
